FAQs

Will I get a certificate?

You will receive a work experience certificate automatically upon completion and a final payment of the platform fees. It will be sent to the email you use for registration in a pdf format.

How much does it cost?

Please see the Pricing Section for more information

Is this a training platform?

No, our platform is for virtual work experience. You will build a project from scratch using your own research and problem solving skills.

Where do I ask for help?

You can get help anytime by leaving a message in our internal chat.

How much time does it take to complete the projects?

Every project has a different difficulty level so it depends on the individual project. You are given 6 months access to the platform to complete as many projects as you can.

Where can I keep up to date with SkillReactor?

To stay up to date with everything we have to offer, follow us on LinkedIn

What tech stack will I be using?

You will be able to choose the technologies you use.
On the frontend you will be able to choose between React, Angular or Vue as your framework.


And on the backend you will be able to choose between Typescript, Javascript or Python.
